Bagman remake by JOTD in 2010 - NDS port


To install

1) create a directory called "data" at the root of your M3/R4/whatever SD card and unzip the contents of "Bagman.zip" here
2) move the "Bagman.nds" rom image in the ROM folder (on M3 it is "NDS")
3) enjoy

Controls:

- joystick: move (turn the DS 90 degrees)
- A: cling to handle/drop item/pick item
- L+R: escape to menu
